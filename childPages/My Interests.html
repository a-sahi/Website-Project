Interests


My hobbies are to dance, sing, read books, and to spend time with my family and friends! There are many books that I enjoyed reading, such as 'The Giver', 'The Nest', 'The Hound of the Baskervilles', and 'The Outsiders'.




There are many sports that I enjoy, such as soccer, badminton, floor hockey, skating, kick ball, catch the flag, and swimming. My most favourite one out of all of them is badminton, because I love playing it with my family and friends since I like the way this game tests the speed of your reaction.




I love listening to music, especially to the new Bollywood songs, because they always boost my mood! I would describe my personality as being caring, funny, helpful, and organized!




My favourite country to go for vacation is India, because my family lives there and it's so much fun to go on small trips with them! Already, I have went to many famous places like the Taj Mahal and the Golden Temple!
