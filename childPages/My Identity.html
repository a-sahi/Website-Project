Who I Am


My name is Aastha Sahi, and I was born in Jalandhar, India, and my nationality is Canadian. I came to Canada when I was two and a half years old. I speak many languages which are English, Hindi, and Punjabi.




My ethnicty is Hindu, which is a religion that believes in karma, dharma, and moksha. Also, there are many cultural Indian cuisines that are so delightful to eat, especially the sweets, like jalebi, gulab jamun, and laddu.




The Hindu culture is full of many kinds of celebrations, like Holi, which is the festival of colours, Diwali, which is the festival of lights, Rakhi, which is the festival that celebrates brotherhood and love, Dussehra, which is the festival that is celebrated as a victory of good over evil, and many more!
